A Short Letter of Kadramahan

"

I know we haven't always seen eye to eye. There were arguments, moments where we stood on completely opposite sides, and times when I went ahead and did things you never approved of — choices that went against everything you told me. I won't pretend those moments didn't happen, because they did, and I know some of them hurt you.

But here's the truth I've always carried — even in the middle of every fight, every disagreement, every time I stubbornly did things my own way — I never stopped looking up to you. You were always the standard, even when I acted like I didn't care about it.

You never gave up on me even when I gave you reasons to. That's not something everyone's Kuya does. That's something only you do.

So today, this is your day, Kuyapots — and you better enjoy it. No excuses, no thinking about others, no being the responsible one for just one day. Today is yours, and you deserve every single second of it. 🎂

— Aira Ganda 🖤
